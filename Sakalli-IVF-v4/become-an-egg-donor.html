Become an Egg Donor

Make a Life-Changing Impact as an Egg Donor

At Sakalli IVF, egg donation is a compassionate way to help others achieve parenthood. By donating your eggs, you provide hope to those facing infertility while receiving financial compensation and personal fulfillment.

Why Become an Egg Donor?

Egg donation is a crucial part of fertility treatments for individuals or couples who cannot conceive using their eggs. By becoming an egg donor, you play an integral role in creating families and giving others the joy of parenthood.

Life-Changing Contribution

Your donation helps individuals and couples facing infertility, LGBTQIA+ families, and single parents fulfill their dreams of starting a family.

Personal Fulfillment

Many donors report feeling a deep sense of satisfaction knowing they have made a meaningful impact.

Ongoing Impact

Your decision to donate eggs can create a ripple effect of joy and fulfillment for generations to come.

Empower Hope

Through egg donation, you enable recipients to experience the journey of pregnancy and childbirth.

Financial Compensation

Donors receive competitive compensation in recognition of their time, effort, and generosity.

Egg Donors Requirements

Eligibility Requirements for Egg Donors

At Sakalli IVF , we follow the highest medical, ethical, and legal standards per Northern Cyprus's 57/2014 Law. Donors must meet specific criteria to ensure a safe and successful process.

  • Aged between 20 and 35 years old.
  • Good health with no chronic illnesses or medical conditions.
  • BMI between 19 and 29.
  • Psychologically healthy and undergo a comprehensive psychological evaluation.
  • Not have a history of psychiatric medication use or substance abuse.
  • Not carry any hereditary genetic diseases.
  • Have healthy ovaries, confirmed through medical assessments.
  • Undergo karyotype analysis to confirm a 46, XX chromosomal structure.

Additional Process Requirements

  • Donors may participate in a maximum of three donation cycles per year to prioritize their health and safety.
  • We use stimulation protocols designed to minimize the risk of Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome (OHSS). Typically, 10 eggs are retrieved per cycle.
  • Donors are required to sign a Donor Information and Donation Consent Form to ensure they fully understand and agree to the process.
  • All donation records are securely stored in a centralized electronic system established by the Authorized Authority.

Our Process

The Egg Donation Process

The structured donation process at Sakalli IVF ensures a safe, ethical, and seamless experience for all donors.

  • Medical History and Physical Exam: A detailed review of your medical and family history.
  • Laboratory Tests: Blood tests to screen for infectious diseases (HIV, Hepatitis B/C) and genetic conditions.
  • Ultrasound Evaluation: To assess ovarian health and ensure suitability for donation.
  • Psychological Assessment: To confirm emotional readiness and stability.
